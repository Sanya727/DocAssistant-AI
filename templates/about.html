About Us

Welcome to our application! We are dedicated to leveraging advanced technologies to make document management and querying more efficient and user-friendly. Our platform is built using:

  • Quart: A powerful backend framework that ensures smooth and efficient server operations.
  • React.js: A popular front-end library for creating dynamic and responsive user interfaces.
  • Langchain: Advanced language models that enable natural language processing and understanding.
  • Chroma: Efficient vector stores for managing and searching document embeddings.

Our team is committed to providing a seamless and intuitive experience for managing and querying documents. Whether you are handling general questions or dealing with specific PDF documents, our application is designed to meet your needs with precision and reliability.

Thank you for choosing our application. We hope it makes your work easier and more productive.
